Our Signature style Coffees

Ramsay Misto

The origins of coffee can be traced back to Ethiopia, where legend has it that a goat herder named Kaldi discovered the stimulating effects of the coffee plant. From there, coffee spread throughout the Arabian Peninsula and eventually to Europe and the Americas. Today, coffee is grown in many different parts of the world, with the most popular varieties including Arabica and Robusta.

Chef's Espresso

Espresso is a concentrated coffee that is made by forcing hot water through finely ground coffee beans under high pressure. It has a strong, bold flavor and a thick, creamy crema on top. Espresso is the base for many coffee drinks, including lattes, cappuccinos, and Americanos.

French Press

A French press, also known as a press pot, is a brewing method where coffee is steeped in hot water and then pressed through a mesh filter. It produces a bold, full-bodied coffee with a slightly gritty texture.

macchiato

A macchiato is an espresso shot with a small amount of frothed milk on top. It has a bold, intense flavor and is often served in a demitasse cup.
